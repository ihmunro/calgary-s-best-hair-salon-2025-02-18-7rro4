Our Story

Founded in 2010 by master stylist Maria Chen, Nice Look began as a small studio dedicated to providing personalized, high-quality hair services to Calgary's diverse community. Through word-of-mouth and consistent excellence, we've grown into the city's most trusted salon while maintaining our commitment to individual attention and artistic innovation.

Our mission is to enhance each client's natural beauty while providing an exceptional salon experience. We believe in continuous education, sustainable practices, and creating a welcoming environment where everyone feels valued and understood. Our team's dedication to craft, customer service, and community has earned us recognition as Calgary's Best Salon for three consecutive years.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical appointment take?

Standard cuts typically take 45-60 minutes, while color services can range from 2-4 hours depending on the complexity. We'll provide a detailed time estimate during your consultation.

What if I'm not satisfied with my service?

We offer complimentary adjustments within two weeks of your service. Your satisfaction is our top priority, and we'll work with you until you're completely happy with your results.

Do you offer consultations before major changes?

Yes, we provide free consultations for all major services. This allows us to understand your goals, assess your hair's condition, and create a personalized treatment plan.

What products do you use?

We use premium professional products from leading brands like Kerastase, Oribe, and Kevin Murphy. All our products are carefully selected for quality and environmental responsibility.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a fee equal to 50% of the scheduled service.
